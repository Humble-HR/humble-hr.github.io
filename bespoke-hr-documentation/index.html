Bespoke HR Documentation

Need help with HR documents?

At Humble HR, we understand that every organisation has unique needs and requirements when it comes to documentation. That's why we offer a personalised service to create tailored HR documents for your business.

Our team of experienced HR professionals will work closely with you to understand your organisation's specific needs and goals. We'll then create customised HR documents that align with your company's values, culture, and legislative requirements.

Our most popular services include the creation of a wide range of HR documents, including:

  • Employee Handbooks: We can create employee handbooks that provide a comprehensive guide to your company policies, procedures, and expectations.
  • Employment Contracts: We can create employment contracts that outline the terms and conditions of employment for your organisation.
  • Job Descriptions: We can create job descriptions that accurately reflect the responsibilities, requirements, and expectations of each role in your organisation.
  • Performance Appraisal Forms: We can create performance appraisal forms that help you evaluate your employees' performance and provide feedback on their strengths and areas for improvement.
  • Termination Letters: We can create termination letters that ensure compliance with relevant laws and regulations, while also maintaining professionalism and protecting your company's reputation.
  • Stand-alone policies: We can create stand-alone policies to reflect your organisational needs and expectations of employees.

Our team is dedicated to providing high-quality HR documentation that meet your specific needs. We take pride in our attention to detail, our commitment to delivering on time, and our exceptional customer service. We will listen to your needs & create bespoke HR documents tailored to your organisations requirements.

All documents designed by Humble HR also align with ISO accreditation requirements, as well as other governing bodies.
